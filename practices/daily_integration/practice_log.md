**The goal:** Build awareness of your practice patterns and track your growth over time.

## Tips for Consistent Tracking

**1. Keep it simple**
- Don't let tracking become a burden
- Start minimal, add detail if desired
- Consistency matters more than perfection

**2. Track immediately**
- Fill out daily log right after practice
- Don't wait until end of day (you'll forget)
- Keep log with your practice space

**3. Review regularly**
- Weekly review builds awareness
- Monthly review shows patterns
- Yearly review reveals transformation

**4. Be honest**
- Track skipped days too
- Note challenges without judgment
- Celebrate successes genuinely

**5. Use it for insight**
- Look for patterns
- Notice what supports your practice
- Identify obstacles
- Celebrate growth
