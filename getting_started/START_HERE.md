## Your First Practice (5 Minutes)

**Right now, wherever you are:**

1. **Sit comfortably** - Chair, floor, bed - doesn't matter
2. **Close your eyes** (or soften your gaze)
3. **Take 3 deep breaths:**
   - Inhale slowly through your nose (count to 4)
   - Hold gently (count to 4)
   - Exhale slowly through your mouth (count to 6)
   - Repeat 3 times

4. **Set an intention:** 
   - Place your hand on your heart
   - Say silently or aloud: *"I am here to serve. I am here to heal. I am here to love."*

5. **Feel into your body:**
   - Notice any sensations
   - No need to change anything
   - Just observe with kindness

6. **Open your eyes slowly**

**Congratulations.** You just completed your first planetary healing practice.

It's that simple. And that profound.
